What is Root Canal Treatment?

Root canal treatment is a procedure that can save a tooth that’s badly infected or causing pain, helping you avoid needing it removed. Inside every tooth is a soft tissue called the pulp — if this becomes damaged due to deep decay, cracks, or trauma, it can lead to discomfort and swelling. During treatment, we gently remove the damaged tissue, clean and shape the inside of the tooth, and seal it to stop infection from returning. Thanks to modern techniques and advanced technology at Prodental Clinic, the process is comfortable and precise. Most importantly, it allows you to keep your natural tooth, protecting your bite and avoiding more complex treatment in the future.

What to Expect: Your Root Canal Journey

A closer look at how we restore and protect your natural tooth using advanced techniques and specialist care.

1

Consultation & Diagnosis

We begin with a thorough examination, including digital X-rays and advanced diagnostics, to assess the tooth and confirm if root canal treatment is needed.

2

Local Anaesthesia

To ensure your comfort, the area is carefully numbed so you won’t feel any pain during the procedure.

3

Cleaning & Shaping the Canals

Using a high-powered microscope, Dr Fadi precisely removes the infected or damaged pulp, then cleans and shapes the root canals to prepare for sealing.

4

Sealing the Tooth

The cleaned canals are filled with a biocompatible material to prevent future infection, and the tooth is sealed to protect it.

5

Final Restoration

In most cases, a crown is recommended to restore strength and function, ensuring your tooth stays protected and looks natural.
